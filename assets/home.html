Benifits of Tree

Trees and plants make us happy. They reduce stress, improve our mood, and increase our life satisfaction. They also help us cope with depression and negative thoughts.Trees and plants clean the air. They remove harmful pollutants such as ozone, carbon monoxide, nitrogen dioxide, and sulfur dioxide from the atmosphere. They also produce oxygen that we breathe Trees and plants prevent erosion and flooding. They stabilize the soil, prevent landslides, and absorb excess water. They also reduce runoff and sedimentation in rivers and lakes2.

Need of Plants

We need trees for many reasons. Trees are essential for our survival and well-being. Here are some of the benefits of trees.Trees produce oxygen, which we need to breathe. A mature leafy tree can produce enough oxygen for 10 people in a year.Trees support biodiversity, by providing habitat, food, and shelter for many species of animals and plants. Trees also maintain ecological balance and prevent the loss of species.
